The Problem

Why Most SMEs Struggle With Finance

Without CFO-level oversight, businesses operate with incomplete information and miss critical opportunities.

01

Junior-Managed Accounts

Financial function handled without senior oversight — leading to errors, blind spots, and strategic misalignment.

02

Compliance Risks

VAT, Corporate Tax, and audit obligations managed reactively — creating exposure to penalties and last-minute stress.

03

Manual, Delayed Reporting

Reports take too long to prepare and are outdated by the time they reach decision-makers.

04

No Cash Flow Visibility

Business owners don't know their real cash position until it becomes a crisis — not a strategy.

05

No Profitability Clarity

Revenue looks healthy but profits are unclear — no visibility into which products, customers, or markets are actually profitable.

06

Finance Isn't Strategic

Accounting is handled but finance is never used as a growth tool — no planning, no forecasting, no strategic direction.
