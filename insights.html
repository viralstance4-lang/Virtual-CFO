Management accounts are prepared internally for business decision-making — they can be monthly, formatted any way you need, and focused on performance. Statutory accounts are formal financial statements prepared for regulatory filing, following accounting standards (IFRS or local GAAP).
At a minimum, monthly. High-growth or cash-intensive businesses should review key metrics weekly. With Power BI dashboards, real-time monitoring becomes possible — allowing you to catch issues and opportunities as they emerge.
UAE Corporate Tax applies to businesses with taxable income exceeding AED 375,000 at a standard rate of 9%. It came into effect for financial years starting on or after June 1, 2023. Free zone entities may qualify for 0% on qualifying income. Proper compliance requires timely registration, accurate accounting, and annual tax return filing.
A solid cash flow forecast includes: opening cash balance, expected receipts from sales/collections, planned payments (suppliers, payroll, overheads, loan repayments), net cash movement per period, and closing cash balance. A 13-week rolling forecast is the gold standard for operational cash management.
Key indicators of accurate accounts include: all bank accounts fully reconciled, AR and AP ledgers matching supporting documents, inter-company balances cleared, fixed assets properly recorded and depreciated, and no unexplained suspense or clearing balances.
